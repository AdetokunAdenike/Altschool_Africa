BIOGRAPHY

My name is Adenike, a mathematics graduate from the Federal University of Technology, Minna, Niger State. Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, I am a first-generation graduate from a family where resilience and hard work are highly valued. These values have shaped me into the determined individual I am today.

Growing up, education wasn’t easily accessible to me at first, but that didn’t stop my thirst for knowledge. I began primary school at the age of 8, and within a year, I was excelling academically, often topping my class and representing my school in debate and quiz competitions. My elder sister, Rukayat, played a significant role in my early learning journey. She would teach me what she learned after her daily tasks, and this laid a strong foundation for my academic success. My uncle, a mathematics teacher, also inspired me by fostering my love for numbers and problem-solving, which later became the cornerstone of my interest in technology.

Throughout my academic journey, I was drawn to computers and intrigued by the limitless possibilities technology offers. As I progressed, my curiosity grew into a passion for software engineering. I was fascinated by the idea of using technology to solve real-world problems, improve systems, and make life easier for others. However, self-learning proved challenging, as I struggled to find structure and guidance in my studies.

Why I joined AltSchool

My determination to succeed led me to taking the bold step to join ALTSCHOOL AFRICA, in order to benefit from structured learning and live, instructor-led classes. This decision has been transformative for me. Not only has it equipped me with practical skills, but it has also allowed me to network with like-minded individuals and gain the confidence to pursue my dreams.

Today, I am focused on building a career in tech, particularly in software engineering. My analytical skills, rooted in my mathematics background, give me a unique perspective in problem-solving and algorithmic thinking. I am driven by the desire to contribute to innovative solutions and make a meaningful impact in the tech industry.

Outside of academics and technology, I am passionate about self-development and continuous learning. I believe that with determination, focus, and the right opportunities, anyone can overcome challenges and achieve their goals.

GOALS

My goal is to become a world-class software engineer with expertise in building scalable, secure, and efficient backend systems while mastering cloud technologies to deploy, manage, and optimize solutions like a professional. I aim to leverage the knowledge gained from the Cloud Engineering program at AltSchool to bridge the gap between my passion for technology and practical skills required to thrive in the tech industry. This program is not just a stepping stone for me; it is the foundation I need to turn my dreams into reality. I want to use my skills to build impactful solutions, drive innovation, and create opportunities that will not only transform my career but also rewrite my family's story, by God’s grace. Ultimately, I want to do what I love—pursuing software engineering and cloud computing—while building a meaningful and fulfilling career that brings value to others and allows me to make a lasting impact in the world of technology.
